## GW Open Data Workshops

The annual **IGWN Open Data Workshop** is the best structured resource for beginners and intermediate students. All materials are freely available.

| Year | Link | Topics |
|---|---|---|
| ODW 2024 | [gw-odw/odw-2024](https://github.com/gw-odw/odw-2024) | Data access, waveforms, matched filtering, PE |
| ODW 2023 | [gw-odw/odw-2023](https://github.com/gw-odw/odw-2023) | Noise, waveforms, parameter estimation |
| ODW 2022 | [gw-odw/odw-2022](https://github.com/gw-odw/odw-2022) | Signal processing, Bayesian inference |
| All years | [gw-odw.thinkific.com](https://gw-odw.thinkific.com/) | Structured online course format |

## Advanced Lecture Series

### Perimeter Institute (PIRSA)
Free recordings of lectures from leading researchers.
- [Search: gravitational waves](https://pirsa.org/search?searchQuery=gravitational+waves)
- [Search: LIGO data analysis](https://pirsa.org/search?searchQuery=LIGO+data+analysis)
- Recommended speakers: K. Chatziioannou, S. Vitale, M. Vallisneri, N. Cornish

### ICTP Schools
- [ICTP Applied Math & Theoretical Physics activities](https://www.ictp.it/research/appmath/activities)
- Search for GW-related schools; recordings and notes are often freely available

### Les Houches Schools
- [Measuring GWs from Binary Black Holes ‚Äì Flanagan & Hughes (2005)](https://arxiv.org/abs/gr-qc/0501016) ‚Äî detailed lecture notes
- [Gravitational Wave Sources ‚Äì school notes (2012)](https://arxiv.org/abs/1209.0310) ‚Äî comprehensive overview

### LISA Training Week (LATW)
- [github.com/mikekatz04/LATW](https://github.com/mikekatz04/LATW/tree/main)
- M. Katz, N. Karnesis, N. Korsakova, A. Sasli, A. Nilsson, R. Tenorio, D. Rai, C. Chapman-Bird (2024)
- Hands-on LISA data analysis tutorials
